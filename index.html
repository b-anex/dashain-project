About This Website

This fan website is a celebration of SZA's artistry, music, and influence. Created as a school project, it aims to connect fans and share the inspiration found in SZA's work.

Why SZA?

SZA's music blends soulful vocals, poetic lyrics, and unique style. Her albums, including "CTRL" and "SOS," have inspired millions and continue to shape the sound of modern R&B.

Project Purpose

This site was built to showcase web design skills and pay tribute to an artist who motivates creativity and authenticity. Explore the gallery, share feedback, and join the fan community!
